    requireNumericLiterals

    Requires use of binary, hexadecimal, and octal literals instead of parseInt.

    Type: Boolean

    Value: true

    Version: ES6

    Example

    "requireNumericLiterals": true
    Valid
    0b111110111 === 503;
0o767 === 503;
0x1F7 === 503;
    Invalid
    parseInt("111110111", 2) === 503;
parseInt("767", 8) === 503;
parseInt("1F7", 16) === 255;
